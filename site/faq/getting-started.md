# Getting started

## I am coming from Chrome. What should I do first?

Start with **Comfortable Mode**, then explore Balanced and Performance modes as you get comfortable. See [Get started](/get-started/) for installation and first run.

## I feel lost. Is that normal?

Yes. Coming from a traditional browser, Phi may feel unusual at first because it is organized around different assumptions. That settles quickly with use.

## What is the fastest way to understand Phi?

Use it as a browser first. Do not try to understand every AI feature on day one — browse normally, and let the rest make sense through use.
